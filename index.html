A quick tour around three of the most vibrant cities in Europe

Paris

Interesting facts:

  • Paris is known as “The City of Lights”.
  • The famous bell of Notre Dame Cathedral is called Emmanuel, and he weighs 13 tons.
  • The famous “Bloody Mary” cocktail originated in Paris at the Ritz Hotel.
Budapest

Interesting facts:

  • Rubik, the inventor of the famous rubik’s cube, was born in Budapest.
  • Budapest has more thermal water springs than any other capital city in the world.
  • Budapest is known for its ‘ruins pubs’.
Copenhagen

Interesting facts:

  • The Danes enjoy salty licorice with a cold glass of milk.
  • The average tax rate in Denmark is 49%.
  • The Copenhagen harbor is clean enough to swim in!
